Signs

"BIRD" - One pointer finger pointed up, other fingers curled.

"TURTLE" - Pointer finger and middle finger pointed up in a "peace sign", other fingers curled.

"FISH" - Three fingers pointed up (pointer, middle, ring), thumb holds down ring finger.

"BUG" - Four fingers pointed up (pointer, middle, ring, pinkie), thumb holds down pinky.

"ANIMAL" - Thumb pointed up, like a "thumbs up", with four other fingers curled (pointer, middle, ring, pinkie)
